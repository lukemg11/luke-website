SOCCER

Favorite team: Manchester United

Favorite player: Bruno Fernandes

Position I play: Center Mid

Goalkeeper The final line of defense and the only player that can use their hands. Definitely the most stressful position.
Defender Defenders try to prevent the attackers from getting past them and potentially scoring. It helps if they are strong and/or fast.
Midfielder Midfielders need to be good at passing. Their main job is to link the defense and the attack. They need to have very good endurance and it helps to have good game IQ.
Attacker Attackers need to be good at finishing, as their main job is simply to score goals. It helps if they are fast and strong.
